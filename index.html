「Elements of AI」

標題

簡介性段落（網站內容）

Elements of AI is a educational website created by a college in Finland called the University of Helsinki.The purpose of the website is to provide free education for those who want or are interested in learning the basics of artificial intelligence which is what we know as AI.

特色

There are two main chapters: part 1 is the introduction of AI and part 2 is building AI. Both of these courses are free.In my opinion ,I think part 1 is for beginners who are just getting started, and part 2 is for those who want to be more advanced with building an AI of their own.

用戶利益

Personally, I think users get a lot of benefits from this website.For example, it gives a starter guide for beginners, and as well as those who want to go deeper in the circle of AI with the course of helping you build yourself an AI of your very own.

用後感想

After trying some of the courses myself, I recommend those who want to get started with AI to try it out. It not only helps you learn more about AI but also helps you learn some English vocabulary while learning.

動機

The reason why I chose this course as my subject to study is because I myself love everything about technology both software and hardware, and as the technology keeps evolving artificial intelligence has become the most popular topic now and not gonna lie it’s pretty convenient with the help of AI. Things have become easier than ever for example Chatgpt is one of the most well known AI chat bot that has come out because it’s so good that microsoft bought it for millions of dollars, you might wonder what makes the huge corporation buy a chat bot? In my opinion, I think the main reason of the decision they make is because the say the enormous amount of money it can make, Chatgpt is capable of writing a essay or a story of anything as long as you gave him a topic, millions of student ask it to write a model essay for them for suggestion only. And think about this, if Microsoft charged people 3 USD a month to use the program it might not seem a lot but with a huge amount of clients, it will surely bring a lot of money.
